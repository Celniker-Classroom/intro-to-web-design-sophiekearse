Softball

I have played softball for 12 years. I played 3 years for my high school's varsity team, and 5 years of travel. I've been with my current travel team for 3 years, and many of my teammates for much longer than that. I catch, play first, and sometimes play the outfield. I love softball, and I spend most of my time outside of school either at practice or games. My teammates are some of my closest friends, and I deeply value my time with them. Last year, I played tournaments in Houston, Sacramento, Portland, and Las Vegas. This year, we're going to Toronto, Canada for an international tournament.
